Identity for the MITES 40th Anniversary Gala

This identity for the MIT Office of Engineering Outreach Program (OEOP) was designed for the MITES 40th Gala.

The MITES 40th timeline was designed as nine related posters that viewers could engage with by adding their own memories by writing on the provided post-it notes. To view the timeline, scroll through (left to right).

The Anniversary logo was adapted from the MITES logo (designed by Opus Design).
