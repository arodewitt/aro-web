Resume



I’m a graphic designer with a fine arts background and experience in higher ed marketing and communications. I live and work out of Boston, MA where I’m a recent graduate in the M.F.A. program in graphic design at Boston University, a some-of-the-time freelancer, and a full-time multitasker. There’s more stuff (if you’re curious) at hooligan mag.

I received my B.A. in Biology and Visual Art from Swarthmore College and have never stopped trying to make science and art inseparable. I’m madly in love with my Kindle and all things yellow ochre.
