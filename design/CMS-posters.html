MIT Comparative Media Studies Course Posters

The MIT Comparative Media Studies department offers several courses each semester, and their posters offer an opportunity to tease the classes to undergraduate and graduate level students.

Starting with the course descriptions and/or syllabi, I work with the department to develop a system that both showcases key elements of each course and visually groups them. Because the posters are often display in MIT's famously busy "infinite corridor" tiling them helps the course posters stand out. Below are posters from the Fall 2016 semester.
